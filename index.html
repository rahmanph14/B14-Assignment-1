Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

What's included in my ticket? +

Every ticket includes access to all keynote and track sessions, networking breaks, and a digital badge. Workshop access, meals, and recordings vary by tier — check the pricing cards above for full details.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +

Yes. Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague at no extra cost.

Is there a virtual attendance option? +

DevConf 2026 is an in-person-only event this year. Pro and Team tickets include lifetime access to session recordings if you're unable to join us on-site.

Do you offer group or student discounts? +

Teams of 10+ should look at our Team pass above. For student discounts, reach out to our support team with a valid .edu email for a verification link.

What's the deadline for early-bird pricing? +

Early-bird pricing shown above is valid through August 15, 2026. Prices increase after that date, so lock in your seat early.
